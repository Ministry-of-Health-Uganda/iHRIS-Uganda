Hariri kadhia ya SQL

Dodosi la Sql:

Kufasiri SQL kwa ajili ya kadhia hii. Unarejea kwenye nyuga zilizoripotiwa kama “Fomu ya ripoti+uga”

Ufafanuzi:

Ufafanuzi wa kadhia hii kuonyeshwa kwa mtumiaji wa mwisho

Uga ya Fomu:

Chagua uga ya fomu ambamo kadhia hii inaingiza thamani

Aggregate:

A comma separated list of the forms in the relationship on whose fields you which you wish to aggregate on (i.e. those not in the groupby clause, these are the forms that appear in your function)

Fomu Zinazochagulika

The forms which are selectable for this field. Only applies if the form field is a subclass of I2CE_FormField_MAPPED. In which case it should be a comma separated list of forms. If not set, it defaults to the name of this function.

Nyuga zilizounganishwa

Nyuga zinaunganishwa wakati wa kuorodhesha chaguzi. Hutumika tu kama uga ya fomu ni daraja dogo la I2CE_Uga ya Fomu_ILIYOPANDIKIZWA.Inapaswa kuwa orodha za 'fomu' zilizotenganishwa na alama ya nukta pacha au 'fomu+kiungo cha uga. Mfano: "nchi:wilaya+mkoa:[mkoa]:nchi".
